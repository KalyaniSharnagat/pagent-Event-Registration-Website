VEC Queen of India: The Ultimate Pageant Experience

____CEO & FOUNDER OF ORGANIZATION____

Muskaan Gairola

(Founder & CEO)

As the visionary entrepreneur and celebrated CEO of Vesna Event Crafters (aka VEC) and VEC Queen of India, with two decades of expertise in the travel, tourism, and events industry, Muskaan has spearheaded innovative approaches that redefine the standards of excellence in event curation and travel experiences.

Muskaan's accolades extend beyond her professional endeavors. Crowned 'Mrs. Charismatic' at Mrs. India Worldwide 2021 and holding the prestigious IFSA Super Woman 2021 Award in the multi-talent category, she embodies grace, intelligence, and versatility.

Her influence transcends traditional boundaries, as evidenced by her roles as a digital creator, model, and collaborator with renowned brands. Muskaan's presence on the runway has been nothing short of mesmerizing, serving as a show stopper at India Style Fashion Week New Delhi 2022 and as a show opener for Indian Maverick Fashion at International Fashion Week Goa 2021. She has also been honored by Milestone Global Award in Lifestyle and Fashion in 2022, a testament to her significant contributions to these industries.
