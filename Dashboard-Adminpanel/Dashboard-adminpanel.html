Welcome Admin

Use this panel to manage event schedules and contestant registrations.

Contestant Applications

Contestant Applications allow you to manage entries submitted by participants. You can view details, approve or reject applications, and export data for records or reports.

Judges

Judges management allows you to add, edit, or remove judges from the system. You can assign them to specific events for evaluation purposes. This ensures fair and organized scoring throughout the competition.

Event Schedule

Event Schedule helps you organize the flow of your pageant or event. You can define different stages, set start/end times, and assign deadlines for tasks or submissions. This ensures smooth coordination and timely execution of all activities.

Media Upload

Media Upload allows contestants and admins to upload event-related photos and videos. You can organize media by category or event for easy access. This feature helps in managing visual content for promotions, galleries, or judging.

Send Notifications

Send Notifications enables you to communicate important updates to participants via email. You can send announcements, reminders, or alerts about events or deadlines. This ensures everyone stays informed and engaged throughout the event.

Blog / News

Blog / News lets you create and manage posts about your events, sponsors, and pageant highlights. Share important news, behind-the-scenes stories, or updates with your audience. This keeps your platform active and helps build community engagement.
