VEC Queen of India (Ms. & Mrs.)

VEC Queen of India is a celebration of every woman’s unique journey, a platform where dreams meet opportunity. Designed for both Ms. and Mrs., this remarkable competition runs two pageants simultaneously, creating a shared space where women from all walks of life can step into the limelight.

This is more than just a pageant—it’s a movement that spans across India, welcoming participants from every corner of the country. Whether you’ve always dreamed of walking the ramp, showcasing your talent, or simply redefining your own boundaries, VEC Queen of India is here to make it happen.

Season 1 was a testament to the magic that happens when passion meets opportunity. With dazzling rounds, unmatched media coverage, and moments of triumph for every participant, VEC Queen of India celebrates confidence, individuality, and the pursuit of dreams.

Proudly organized by Vesna Event Crafters Pvt. Ltd., part of the illustrious Vesna Group with a rich legacy in the travel and events industry, VEC Queen of India reflects the group’s commitment to creating transformative and memorable experiences. At VEC Queen of India, we don’t just crown winners; we create legacies—a tradition upheld by Vesna’s decades of expertise and excellence.

Don't miss this chance to shine! Register now to VEC Queen Of India 2025 and start your journey to Triumph!

Muskaan Gairola

(Founder & CEO)

As the visionary entrepreneur and celebrated CEO of Vesna Event Crafters (aka VEC) and VEC Queen of India, with two decades of expertise in the travel, tourism, and events industry, Muskaan has spearheaded innovative approaches that redefine the standards of excellence in event curation and travel experiences.

Muskaan's accolades extend beyond her professional endeavors. Crowned 'Mrs. Charismatic' at Mrs. India Worldwide 2021 and holding the prestigious IFSA Super Woman 2021 Award in the multi-talent category, she embodies grace, intelligence, and versatility.

Her influence transcends traditional boundaries, as evidenced by her roles as a digital creator, model, and collaborator with renowned brands. Muskaan's presence on the runway has been nothing short of mesmerizing, serving as a show stopper at India Style Fashion Week New Delhi 2022 and as a show opener for Indian Maverick Fashion at International Fashion Week Goa 2021. She has also been honored by Milestone Global Award in Lifestyle and Fashion in 2022, a testament to her significant contributions to these industries.
